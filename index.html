Things I've worked on

Real-rootedness Conjectures in Matroid Theory. My bachelor's thesis, in which I write about two conjectures in matroid theory—that the Hilbert series of matroid Chow rings and augmented Chow rings are real-rooted—and address special cases of these and a third related conjecture. This project was proposed by June Huh, who had suspected that real-rootedness might hold no earlier than 2019, and was co-advised by Huh and Ravi Vakil.
